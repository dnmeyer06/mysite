Do you know how much food is thrown away every day in the United States? Do you?!?! Seriously, do you? A statistic would be really compelling here. But it's a butt-ton, that's for sure. For that reason our head chef and founder, Rupert, has poineered the concept of "reclaimed food." Every meal that you consume at Pretension will included at least 20% food that has been recovered from the refuse heap. You can feel superior in your knowledge that your food would have gone to waste. While some may winge at "health-code violations" and "infection from exposure to waste", we know that you have thrown off such square notions and will fully embrace the culinary venture that you are about to embark upon.

When the time comes, the mixture of emotions in the cow will provide the most succulent taste to your burger
